💀 The Origin of “Deadline”

Before Deadline Astrology became a brand, it was a band name—the very first one created by Nickolas Kettell, now known as Saint Nicky JK. Sitting in his grandmother’s house on the corner of his childhood street, he wrote the word "Deadline" in red ink on lined loose-leaf paper. The inspiration? Hospital heart monitors.

Nick had been hospitalized nine times as a child—one stay ending with him being defibrillated back to life. He awoke in a white room, as if he had just returned from the Moon, with Mary Poppins playing on the hospital TV. He watched them clean chimneys while singing "chim chimeny, chim chim cher-ee." That surreal moment between worlds birthed the name “Deadline.”

👼 From Atheist to Believer

Before that experience, Nicky was a firm atheist. Spirituality, the unseen, the paranormal—it all seemed like fantasy. But on January 13, 2013, 13 days before his 17th birthday, his mother passed away. As they pulled the plug on her life support, a white light filled the hospital room and gave him a message: “Sing over your guitar.”

The next day, he wrote a song that doubled as a séance ritual. As he performed it in his Nan’s basement, a sparkling, angelic presence appeared and gently touched his left arm. It was his mother. She had returned to let him know everything would be okay. From that moment, his worldview shattered and reassembled—this time rooted in the spiritual and unseen.
